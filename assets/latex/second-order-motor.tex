        G(s) = \frac{K_t}{(Ls + R)(Js + B) + K_t K_b} \quad \left[ \frac{\text{rad/s}}{\text{V}} \right]
    \]

    where: \\
        \begin{tabular}{l l}    
            $K_t$: Torque Constant $\left( \frac{N \cdot m}{A} \right)$ \\
            $K_b$: Back EMF Constant $\left( \frac{rad/s}{V} \right)$ \\
            $L$: Motor Inductance $(H)$ \\
            $R$: Motor Resistance $(\Omega)$ \\
            $J$: Rotor Inertia $(\text{kg}\cdot\text{m}^2)$ \\
            $B$: Viscous Friction Constant $(\text{N}\cdot\text{m}\cdot\text{s})$ \\
        \end{tabular}
